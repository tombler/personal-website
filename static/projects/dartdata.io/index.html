THE PROBLEM

"the raindrop effect"

There can be many inconsistencies in the presentation of metadata. In this music-related example of Chopin's Prelude No. 15 "Raindrops", there are over 700 total entries with 334 unique entries that refer to the same piece. The result: confusion and an inability to find the correct recording. Poor user experiences for the consumer cost time and money. Record labels, rights organizations, distributors and others trying to reconcile and properly account for activity waste time and effort.

THE SOLUTION

"Out of Many, One"

Dart normalizes the metadata into the correct formatting, making the song title easily identifiable. This leads to improved discoverability, efficient workflows and accurate reporting.
